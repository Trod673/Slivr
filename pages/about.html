What is Slivr?

Slivr is a way to efficiently plan and track your routine. It will work directly with your google calendar to allow for easy import of events for planning your day. In addition, if your friends use Slivr as well, it will analyze when you are both free, allowing you to optimize your free time. Finally, weather you're using Slivr with friends or without, you will be able to analyze your routine and see if you are using your time effectively.

Why the name Slivr?

When I would track my schedules in college, each day was a sliver of my week when I drew it out. With each day being your sliver, and comparing your sliver to your friends', I came up with the name Slivr.

What's the point?

Getting into a routine and planning your day help fight depression and anxiety. I wanted a way to conveniently plan my day, but also still be able to meet up with my friends. This accomplishes that by having you plan your day, reflect on the previous, and by telling you when you and your friends can meet!

Get ready to enjoy your time again.
